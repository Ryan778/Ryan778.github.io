Did you know that you can view your schedule even though it's not available on the public SVUE page anymore?

This page will let you view your schedule for next year ahead of time in two easy steps!

Step 1: Login to SVUE below the same way you normally login.
Step 2: Once logged in, press the blue "Load My Schedule!" button. You should see your classes after a few seconds!

Note: If you see a login page after pressing "Load My Schedule!", then you're not logged in. If it happens repeatedly, reloading the page usually fixes it.
