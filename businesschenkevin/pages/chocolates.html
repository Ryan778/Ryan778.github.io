  How We Make Our Chocolates:  
     
     
We make our chocolates everyday from fresh cocoa seeds and sell our chocolates immediately. On our online shop, our chocolates are shipped fresh guaranteed.  
     
We use fresh shipments of cocoa seeds to guarantee a natural, fresh taste. We peel the seeds ourselves and grind them into the natural taste that you feel melting into your mouth. Our chocolates are fresh, guaranteed, and if you're dissatisfied, we will always offer a refund.
